## 🔧 Troubleshooting & Known Issues

### ❌ Executor Won't Inject

- Temporarily disable your antivirus during injection — it may be blocking the process
- Verify you're using the **latest version** from the [releases page](https://rblxdevz.github.io/rblx/)

### ❌ iOS App Crashes on Launch

- Go to **Settings → General → Device Management** and re-trust the developer certificate
- If AltStore shows the app as expired, open AltStore and click **Refresh All**
- Reinstall the **roblox executor ipa** after revoking the old certificate in settings
- Ensure your iOS version meets the minimum requirement (iOS 14+)

### ❌ Android App Says "Parse Error"

- The APK may have downloaded incompletely — delete and re-download from the official source
- Ensure your Android version is 8.0 or higher
- Check that "Install Unknown Apps" is enabled for your browser/file manager app

### ❌ Script Executes But Nothing Happens

- The script may be outdated — check if the popular title it targets received a recent update
- Open the Console panel and look for error messages — Lua errors will appear there
- Try a simple test script like `print("hello")` first to confirm the executor is working correctly

### ❌ Config File Changes Not Saving

```bash

# Fix write permissions if needed
```

### ❌ High Memory Usage

- Enable `low_ram_mode: true` in your `config.json`
- Close other background applications before launching
